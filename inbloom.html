INSPIRATION

The reason why I chose this topic was for my friends. I have a few good friends who are in long distance relationships. They always have communication conflicts when they contact their partners because of the long distance.

PROBLEMS

People who are in long distance relationships find:
1. Their mood is easily ignored by their partners.
2. They can’t express emotions by messaging only.
3. Lack of interaction with their partners.

SOLUTION

My solution is to create an app on the smartphone that will helps couples to solve communication problems to build their affection in a fun and meaningful way.
